About Me

MERN stack Developer with experience in architecture and interior design. Bachelors degree in Architecture and a certification in full stack development from the University of Minnesota.

Current position is a Store Planner for Target headquarters. I work with teams of designers, architects, and facilities management to update Target stores as the company grows and evolves to best serve its guests. I currently support the food service team and work on a variety of projects in grocery, Starbucks, and Snack Bar.

Outside of the professional environment I enjoy CrossFit, fostering dogs from Midwest Animal Rescue, and working part time at VolksWagon when I am not attending school. I enjoy visiting new places, home improvement projects, and learning new skills such as guitar, sewing, painting, etc.
